About Me

I am a psychologist who specializes in sex therapy and couple therapy. That being said, I have always been a computer geek and loved technology, games, and coding. My first career was a Java developer. Since beginning my doctoral program in 2002 I have been thinking about what contribution I could bring to integrating these seemingly disparate fields. What you will find here are the fruits of my labor.
